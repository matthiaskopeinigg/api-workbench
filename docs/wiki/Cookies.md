# Cookies

> **Maintainers:** Sync to [GitHub Wiki](https://github.com/matthiaskopeinigg/api-workbench/wiki).

## Opening the cookie manager

Click the **cookie** icon in the **title bar** (next to Settings). A **Cookie manager** modal opens.

## Purpose

Inspect and manage **HTTP cookies** used by outbound requests from the workbench (domain/path scoped storage as implemented). Useful when debugging sessions, OAuth flows, or sticky load balancers.

## Related

- [Request editor](Request-editor) — sends use stored cookies according to request options (e.g. “use cookies” when present).
- [Settings](Settings) — SSL/proxy affect how cookies are sent to hosts.
